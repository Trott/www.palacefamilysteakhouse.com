About Palace Family Steak House

Posted by Box Daddy

Palace Family Steak House is a globally-recognized maximum clearinghouse for the Study of Institutional Studies and the Performance of the Production of Culture. Palace Family Steak House is the premiere home for culturally-charged non-cultural products and processes.

We are pleased to have had the opportunity to produce numerous semi-cultural products in vernacular and non-vernacular guises, including several excellent issues of the Palace Family Steak House online journal. In addition, the organization has been deeply committed to the production of important works of multimedia spectacle, such as Steak House! (The Musical) and the recently withdrawn photo-novel Liberals, A Novel.

Palace Family Steak House is not to be confused with the Palace Steak House which, like Palace Family Steak House, is located in the historic Mission District of San Francisco, California. The main difference between the two is that Palace Family Steak House is a semi-cultural organ and production center for institutional products, while Palace Steak House is in fact a steakhouse.

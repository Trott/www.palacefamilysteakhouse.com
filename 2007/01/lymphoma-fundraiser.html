"One Night Stand" Fundraiser @ Slim's

Posted by Trott


I don't know who this guy is.Although the Palace Family Steak House band doesn't currently have another show scheduled until March, I'll be participating in the 10th Annual One Night Stand at Slim's in San Francisco on Saturday, January 20.

This is a benefit show for the Cancer Lymphoma Research Society. Local music notables form cover/tribute bands for the evening to play up to three songs each.

Come support a great cause and watch me play with current/former members of bands that are much more well-known than my own.

More details are available here.

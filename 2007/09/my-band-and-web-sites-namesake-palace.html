For Sale: One Namesake

Posted by Trott

My band and web site's namesake, the Palace Steak House in San Francisco, is for sale. The building (which includes the steak house and four apartments) can be yours for just under $2 million.

I pledge that if I raise $2 million through the band between now and the end of the year, I will purchase the Palace Steak House!

Help launch me on my way to raising $2 million dollars by coming to Bottom of the Hill on Wednesday, September 19 to see the Palace Family Steak House band perform!

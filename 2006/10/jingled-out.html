Jingled Out

Posted by Trott


Above photo of baby back ribs taken from:
PorkFoodService.Com
No really: PorkFoodService.ComWith a Snapple jingle, a Bagel Radio jingle, and a Palace Family Steak House jingle all under my belt, you think I'd jump at a competition to rewrite a Chili's jingle. But no. It doesn't present much in the way of opportunities to rework it. There's no melody to be placed into a different context. I guess one could use Latin percussion and sing it in Spanish.

Of the original Chili's jingle, PFSH reader and fan Stephanie Brown says: "I hate that song more than most things I hate."

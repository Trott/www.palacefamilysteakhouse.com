The 6-Hi Project Goes To Sundance

Posted by Trott

I spent days 7-9 of the 6-Hi Project in Utah for the Sundance Film Festival. I saw five movies in slightly more than 24 hours, and I discovered that Utahns are more receptive to a friendly "hi" than San Franciscans. Or maybe it was just something in the water because I've continued to have good results with the whole "hi" thing since returning to San Francisco. If my "it's in the water" theory is accurate, than my good luck should end tomorrow after I evacuate my bladder.

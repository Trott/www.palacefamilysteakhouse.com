Guest Editorial by Ed "Too Tall" Jones

Posted by Lynn Swann

As we all know, athletes tend to be conservatives while actors tend to be liberals.

So, it comes as no surprise that Billy "White Shoes" Johnson attacks Republican football great Lynn Swann and defends Communist Charles Box Daddy. Billy always was more of an actor than a true athlete, what with that silly touchdown dance of his and all.

I guess Billy's just insecure and jealous, since he was never selected for the cover of a black history book published by the Urban League. (Congratulations, Lynn!)

I'm also sure that Billy doesn't get billing next to heroes like astronaut James Lovell in illuminating articles by intelligent men like Dr. Steven Keteyian. It is sad to watch Billy translate his own insecurity into jealousy of the successful, strong Lynn Swann.

Other Too Tall political notes: Lynn has given $2000 to John Kerry's Senate campaigns. I guess that happens, even when you're a Bush-loving Republican, when you have close ties with the Heinz family. He's also given $8800 over the years to various Republican campaigns.

And while we're talking about Pennsylvania politics, does anyone really believe that Rick Santorum isn't hoping to run for president if he wins his 2006 Senate re-election bid? Come on, Rick! We're not stupid!

Go Fighting Au Jus!

Yours truly,
Ed "Too Tall" Jones

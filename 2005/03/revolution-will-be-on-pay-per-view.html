The Revolution Will Be On Pay-Per-View

Posted by Trott

The critics are unanimous about the Palace Family Steak House theme song:

Rich Trott, you have a lot to answer for. I woke up this morning singing the Palace Family Steak House theme song. Could not get it out of my head. I sang it to Ella and she got it stuck in her head too.


And:

I too have had that fucking song stuck in my head for the past 3 days. My coworkers haven't even heard the song and they're sick of it. I keep walking by cubicles singing "It's understood... the food's no good... but there's no place I'd rather be... at the Palace Family Steak Hooowwwwwwse..." Somebody post up the Macarena quick so I can get something else in my head.

"Who let the dogs out" will also work just fine.



So, for those of you that need something to detatch the irresistible pop hook of the Palace Family Steak House theme song from your brain, or if you just simply prefer the ranting style of Coffee Bear (Needs Coffee Now), here is The Revolution Will Be On Pay-Per-View.

(Sorry; I couldn't get the rights to post "The Macarena" or "Who Let The Dogs Out?")

Sitting Around The Apartment All Day In Our Meat Suits

Posted by Trott


We are playing a free show on April 26 at the Hemlock Tavern in San Francisco! We go on before the Slow Poisoner and after Warren Jackson Hearne!

There's a new song, "We Can't Be Stopped", over at our MySpace page.

And, since no one uses MySpace anymore, you should totally become a fan on Facebook.

This morning, Humuhumu and I got to talk to my nephews in Pennsylvania over Skype video chat. I believe it was their first time using video chat. To make it special, Humuhumu put on the bacon suit and I put on the steak suit before we answered the call. We both acted like this is what we do on a typical weekend day--sit around the apartment in our meat costumes.

Joy of Cooking recipes that have been made since the last Joy of Cooking update:
baked artichoke dip, seven-layer dip, hummus and a hummus variant called Half Moon hummus (pictured, photo by Humuhumu).
